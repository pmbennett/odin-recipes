Ice Cream Sundae

Description

Ice cream sundaes are a favorite in homes all across America, and for good reason. They are very customizable, allowing you to use many different flavors of ice creams and add many different toppings.

For this recipe, you'll need a glass or bowl and a big appetite.

Ingredients

  • Ice cream (preferred flavors)
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Caramel syrup
  • Chopped nuts
  • Chocolate chips
  • Fruit
  • Whipped Cream
  • Any additional toppings you'd like

Steps

  1. Add ice cream into bowl, using as many different flavors as you like.
  2. Add whipped cream and syrup.
  3. Add nuts, chocolate chips, and/or fruit.
  4. Add any other toppings you'd like, and enjoy!
