Classic Maragrita

Description

The Margarita is a classic cocktail and is a favorite in Mexico and the United States as well. This classic cocktail goes well with tacos, or on its own to help unwind at the end of the day.

For those of you that love citrus, this is the drink for you! Make sure to have a chilled glass and some rock salt ready to go.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz tequila (blanco works best)
  • 1/2 oz Triple Sec
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • Ice
  • Rock salt (optional)
  • Lime to garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Add ice, tequila, lime juice, and Triple Sec to a cocktail shaker.
  2. Rim your glass with rock salt if you wish, otherwise just have your glass ready.
  3. Shake all ingredients well, and strain into the glass.
  4. Garnish with lime and enjoy!
