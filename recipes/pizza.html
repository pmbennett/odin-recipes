Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This pizza is a surefire favorite for the whole family. Kids and adults alike love it. Originating from Sicily and Italy, this is an easy recipe to make and is fun to make too!

This recipe will make an 18" pizza, enough to comfortably feed a family of four. Make sure to have a sizeable pizza pan or pizza stone ready, and preheat that oven!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Cheese (Mozzerella/Provolone blend
  • Pepperoni (duh)
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Roll the dough out on the pizza pan, poke holes in it all around with a fork to ensure no bubbles form while cooking, and cover it with olive oil, salt, and pepper to taste.
  3. Layer on the sauce to your liking, making sure to cover all the dough.
  4. Add the Mozzerella/Provolone blend to an amount of your liking.
  5. Add the pepperoni (be generous!).
  6. Place into the oven and let cook for 8-12 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
  7. Enjoy!
